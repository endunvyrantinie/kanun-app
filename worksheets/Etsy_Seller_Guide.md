# Etsy Seller Guide: Selling Malaysian HR Worksheets

## Introduction

This guide provides you with everything you need to successfully list and market your new HR worksheets on Etsy. These products tap into a high-demand niche: **Malaysian Labour Law Compliance**. Your target audience includes HR professionals, small business owners, law students, and corporate trainers in Malaysia.

---

## Product Listings

### Product 1: The EA 1955 Essentials Quiz
*   **Title:** Malaysian HR Law Quiz: EA 1955 Essentials (Wages, Hours, Leave)
*   **Description:** A comprehensive 12-question quiz covering the fundamental provisions of the Employment Act 1955. Includes detailed legal explanations and citations. Perfect for HR training and compliance checks.
*   **Tags:** Malaysia HR, EA 1955, Labour Law, HR Training, Employment Act, Compliance Worksheet.

### Product 2: Termination & Misconduct Masterclass
*   **Title:** Malaysian Employment Law: Termination & Misconduct Masterclass
*   **Description:** Master the legal nuances of employee termination and misconduct in Malaysia. Includes scenarios on domestic inquiries, Industrial Relations Act claims, and statutory contributions.
*   **Tags:** Employee Termination, Misconduct Malaysia, HR Masterclass, Industrial Relations, Labour Law Quiz.

### Product 3: East Malaysia Compliance Guide
*   **Title:** Sabah & Sarawak Labour Law Guide: Compliance Quiz & Worksheet
*   **Description:** Navigate the distinct labour ordinances of East Malaysia. Covers Sabah (Cap. 67) and Sarawak (Cap. 76) specific rules on wages, hours, and leave. Essential for businesses operating in East Malaysia.
*   **Tags:** Sabah Labour Law, Sarawak HR, East Malaysia, Labour Ordinance, HR Compliance, Malaysia Law.

## Turning These into Editable Canva Templates

To sell these as "Editable Templates" (which is very popular on Etsy), follow these steps:

1.  **Open Canva:** Create a new "A4 Document" design.
2.  **Copy Content:** Copy the text from the provided Markdown files into Canva.
3.  **Style:** Use professional fonts (e.g., Montserrat, Open Sans) and a clean layout with your "Kanun" branding.
4.  **Share Link:** Once designed, click "Share" > "Template Link" in Canva.
5.  **Delivery PDF:** Create a simple "Thank You" PDF for your Etsy customers that contains the Canva Template Link.
